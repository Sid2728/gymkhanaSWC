Welcome to The Students Senate Council, IIT Guwahati

Students Gymkhana Council is the body that promotes the objectives of fostering extra-curricular, co-curricular activities, welfare of students, and their stay in the campus. It comprises of 5 boards:

  • Technical Board
  • Sports Board
  • Welfare Board
  • Cultural Board
  • HAB
